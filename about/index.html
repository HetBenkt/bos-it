About the company

“Bos ICT Services” found its way on the market in the year 2022 by me, Antal Bos. After 15 years of experience in the field as a Sr. Technical Consultant it became time to bring my knowledge to a next level as owner of my own company.

The primary goals for the company are currently related to the OpenText AppWorks Suite. This means:

  • › Building low-code solutions
  • › Giving advice on best practices
  • › Steering the customer requirements fitting the platform
  • › Integrating with legacy systems
  • › Helping on general maintenance, migration, data conversion, upgrading, and troubleshooting
  • › System and server management with monitoring and reporting
  • › Installing the software and improving the configuration settings
  • › Providing training sessions
  • › Creating HTML5 customization
  • › Building Java modules and extensions
